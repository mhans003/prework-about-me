Introduction

I am a 31-year-old primary school educator who has been in education for 8 years. A bit over a year ago, I finsihed my administrative credential and have participated in leadership roles at my school site. I have been long facing that internal question of, "Is this REALLY what I want to be doing?" I have been truly learning web development and coding in general since 2014, which is not long after I began teaching. Since then, I have completed two certification programs (one through UCR Extension and one through MSJC) in web development and programming, respectively. Since the completion of these two programs, I have continued my self-learning journey through making small math applications, learning newer technologies including Node.js and Mongodb, which have allowed me to synthesize my smaller projects into deployed applications. I am excited to start this long-awaited journey in finally making this career transition by taking a true "bootcamp" experience that will help me to "put it all together" and propel myself forward into a new industry!
